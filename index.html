What's involved?

As an urban volunteer fire brigade, New Brighton responds to all the same kind of call outs as our career counterparts.
This includes anything from structure and vegitation fires to medical calls and even the odd animal rescue.

To ensure we are maintining our skills and fufilling our responsibility to the public, all members of New Brighton Volunteer Fire Brigade are required to:

  • Attend weekly trainings on a Monday evening.
  • Ensure we are avaliable from Friday evening until Monday morning every third week.
  • Maintain a minimum average of 40% call out attendance.

Our members must live in the area. Do you live within 2km of the station?

Find out more

How much training is involved?

You will be trained on station, during our Monday eveining trainings, for at least six months.
Then, once you are ready, you will attend the seven day Recruit Course at Woolston Training Center to attain the rank of Firefighter.
